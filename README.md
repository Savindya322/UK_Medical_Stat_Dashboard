# Created Dashboard using R to analyse the health trends in United Kingdom from 1960 to 2024
# Indicators Considered
•	Demographic & Fertility Indicators
  1. Total Fertility Rate
  2. Crude Birth Rate
  3. Population Pyramid
•	Mortality & Life Indicators
  1. Life Expectancy at Birth
  2. Infant & Under 5 Mortality Rates
•	Epidemiological Trends (Incidence and Prevalence)
  1. Incidence of Tuberculous
  2. Prevalence of overweight of children under 5
•	Healthcare System
  1. Hospital Beds(per 1000 people)
  2. Current Health Expenditure(% of GDP)
